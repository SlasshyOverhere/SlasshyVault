# SlasshyVault Design System

## Colors (OKLCH)
- **Neutral**: `oklch(15% 0.01 250)` (Deep base), `oklch(25% 0.01 250)` (Subtle surface).
- **Accent**: `oklch(75% 0.15 85)` (Warm amber for primary actions), `oklch(65% 0.12 250)` (Deep blue for secondary hints).
- **Functional**: `oklch(70% 0.15 150)` (Success/Watched), `oklch(60% 0.2 20)` (Destructive).

## Typography
- **Headings**: Modern Serif (e.g., Georgia or custom) for show titles to evoke a cinematic feel.
- **UI Text**: High-quality Sans-serif (Inter/Geist) for legibility.
- **Metadata**: Monospace or Condensed Sans for technical details.

## Layout & Rhythm
- **Spacing**: 4px base unit. Consistent 24px or 32px padding for modals.
- **Radius**: `1.5rem` for cards/modals, `0.75rem` for buttons.
- **Elevation**: Multi-layered shadows for depth.

## Components
- **Buttons**: Pill-shaped or soft-rect, heavy on hover states.
- **Episode Cards**: Horizontal layout, 16:9 thumbnails, integrated actions on hover.
- **Sidebar**: Integrated, not floating. Use semantic grouping.
