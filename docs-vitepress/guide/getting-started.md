# Quick Start

1. **Download & install** — Get the latest installer from [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/SlasshyOverhere/SlasshyVault/releases). Requires Windows 10+. MPV is bundled.

2. **Sign in with Google** — The app redirects you to Google for OAuth. This grants access to list and stream your Drive media.

3. **Index your Drive** — In Settings, add cloud folders or scan your Drive. The app indexes metadata locally.

4. **Browse and play** — Your library populates with posters and metadata. Click any title to play.

For Watch Together setup, see [Watch Together](/guide/watch-together).
